First of all:

As part of a project to be completed for our fourth semester at EPITA, we wanted to get started on the concept of breaking the password.


For this reason we wanted to study more deeply the concept of Jonh The Ripper as well as that of Hashacat.


We are four students in the second year of the preparatory cycle at EPITA and are more than motivated to work in groups and thus to enrich our knowledge.


Below you can see the logo of our group, the "SupA1 + 1". The term "Sup" represents the first year of the preparatory cycle at Epita, and we decided to call it this way because the three members of the group were in the same class in the first year and was joined in the second year by a newcomer in our school (hence the "+1").
